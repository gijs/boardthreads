How it works

Emails received on a certain address are immediately sent to Trello. Threaded emails are kept within the same Trello card. Comments prefixed with the :email: emoji, , will be sent back to the submitter, as replies. Markdown is supported.

Comments without the prefix are not sent back, so you can discuss the issues internally.

Try it now

You can get a quick glance of BoardThreads simplicity using just a few clicks and right now: send an email to test@boardthreads.com, then breath deeply and watch as it appear in this board, after that you can reply in the card with a comment (prefixed by the :email: emoji, ) and you'll get the response in your email. Done.
